Send Read Receipts
The Send Read Receipts feature enables others in the conversation to see when you have read their latest message.

Tap the Off/On switch to enable or disable this feature for this conversation.

Share My Location
The Share Location feature enables others in the conversation to track your location. When turned on, each message you send includes your location.

To enable this feature, tap the "On" switch. Choose from among the options presented to set a length of time to share your location, from one hour to indefinitely.

Stop sharing your location by tapping the "Off" switch or by choosing the "Stop Sharing My Location" option from the list.

Note that you can send your location once in the Messages view, without turning on Share Location.

To send your location once, tap the Options button next to the text input to see the Options toolbar. Tap the Location icon, and a list of location options is presented. Choose "Send My Current Location".

Do Not Disturb
The Do Not Disturb feature enables you to stop receiving notifications of new messages for a period of time.

When the "On" switch is tapped, a list of options is presented. Choose from among the options to set a length of time to stop recieving notifications of new messages, from one hour to indefinitely.

Turn off Do Not Disturb to start receiving new message notifications again by tapping the "Off" switch.

Note that setting Do Not Disturb to "Indefinitely" means: "Never notify me of new messages".

Clear Conversation
The Clear Conversation button deletes all messages in this conversation. This action cannot be undone.

When the Clear Conversation button is tapped, a confirmation is presented to be sure you really want to delete all messages in the current conversation.

Confirm delete all messages by tapping "Clear Conversation", or tap "Cancel" to dismiss the confirmation.
